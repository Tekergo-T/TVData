This in-house collective agreement applies to all tariff employees having an employment relationship with Dataport.

This agreement is negotiated between Dataport represented by the Executive Board on the one hand and union representatives of both the United Services Union (ver.di) and the German Federation of Civil Servants and Collective Bargaining Union (dbb) on the other.

The in-house collective agreement was concluded in 2008 on the basis of the TV-L for the state of Schleswig-Holstein and has been negotiated independently of this ever since.
