Introducing the Gross Net Salary Calculator, specifically tailored for public sector employees in Germany. Effortlessly estimate your net salary by selecting your rate, taxes, social security and type of supplementary pension insurance. The calculator will give you an accurate picture of your net salary, along with a table and charts summarizing the taxes and social insurance contributions deducted. It's fast, user-friendly and based on the German income tax system. Need help filling out a specific field? Just hover over the icon for more hints.
